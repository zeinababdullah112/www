Something About Us

At TalkNest, we believe in the transformative power of knowledge and the importance of connecting people with expert speakers. Our mission is simple: to make learning and networking easier by providing a seamless experience for both speakers and learners. Whether you're a professional speaker looking to expand your reach or a learner searching for valuable insights, we are here to bridge that gap.
We understand the significance of visibility in today's competitive world, which is why we've created a platform that not only facilitates learning but also enhances professional exposure. Through our Free Speaker Directories, we help speakers maximize their visibility, offering them the opportunity to reach a wider audience. By providing Business Listings, we ensure that professionals can showcase their expertise, helping them connect with those who can benefit from their knowledge.
In today's digital world, online listings have become a critical tool for professional engagement. By listing speakers in our directory, we aim to elevate the exposure of talented individuals and empower them to grow their networks. We know that every connection made through TalkNest is an opportunity for both speakers and learners to grow, share, and create impact.

Your Gateway to Key Educational

Beyond simply connecting speakers and learners, TalkNest also helps you stay on top of the latest and most important events that can aid you in both your academic and professional life. Whether it's an educational seminar, a career-building workshop, or a networking event with top-tier experts, you'll have access to all the information you need. We keep you updated with the most relevant news about events that matter, hosted by the best speakers, so you never miss an opportunity to learn, grow, and connect.
Join us today and experience how TalkNest is:

  • Reshaping the way knowledge is shared.
  • Transforming how connections are made.
  • Revolutionizing the discovery of events.

Our Story

1-

Brainstorming for Student Events

The idea for the project came to us after a brainstorming session, and we decided to help students just like we were helped during our university activities. We noticed that one of the biggest challenges we faced was finding speakers for the events we were organizing. So, we thought, why not create a website that gathers all the speakers in one place?

2-

Connecting Students with Speakers

We thought about directly connecting students with speakers, making it easier for them to access information about the speakers and their contact details in a simple and straightforward way.

3-

Helping Students with Events

But that wasn't all. We decided to add another feature: providing students with information about upcoming events, so they could easily find and participate in them.

4-

Your Event Planning Assistant

Through this platform, we've been able to help students organize their events more easily and quickly. This has helped us build a strong community of speakers and students interested in student events.

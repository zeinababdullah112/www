Platform Policies

  • Platform Commission

    We charge a 10% commission on the total amount paid to any Speaker or Trainer through our platform. This fee covers the service of connecting clients with the right experts and facilitating the booking process.

  • Booking & Payment

    Payments can be made through the platform or via direct transfer depending on the selected package or agreement.

    The platform is not responsible for any off-platform agreements or payments made without our supervision.

    To confirm a booking, a deposit or full payment may be required depending on the speaker's policy.

  • Cancellation & Refund Policy

    If the client cancels more than 48 hours before the scheduled session, a refund is possible minus a 10% service fee.

    If the client cancels within 48 hours, no refund will be issued.

    If the speaker cancels, the client is entitled to a full refund or rescheduling at no extra charge.

  • Quality Assurance

    We strive to deliver a top-tier experience, but the platform is not liable for the performance or content delivered by speakers. All speakers are rated by client feedback. We reserve the right to suspend or remove any speaker with repeated negative reviews.

  • Code of Conduct

    Any form of abuse, harassment, or unprofessional behavior is strictly prohibited.

    Users who violate these guidelines may be banned from using the platform.

  • Data Privacy & Protection

    We respect your privacy. Personal information will not be shared with any third party without your consent.

    Booking data may be used for analytical and platform improvement purposes only.

  • Intellectual Property

    All content and materials on the platform are copyrighted. You may not reproduce, distribute, or use any material without prior written permission.
